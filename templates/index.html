About

About

I’m Manideep Chandragiri, a visual storyteller and former head of Chitrakala, a college creative club.

I have created 40+ short-form video projects along with hands-on experience in traditional filmmaking, including short films and college documentaries.

My work focuses on storytelling, pacing, and visual consistency, combining filmmaking fundamentals with modern creative tools.

I’m adaptable to different styles, formats, and creative requirements, and I enjoy exploring new approaches to visual storytelling.

Currently, I’m focused on refining my craft and building stronger, more structured visual narratives.

More work and ongoing projects can be found on YouTube and Instagram.
